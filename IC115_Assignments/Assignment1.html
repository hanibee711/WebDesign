Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique
Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of these portraits in the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil On Canvas
Dimensions: 100x70cm
Home: Musèe du Louvre, Paris
